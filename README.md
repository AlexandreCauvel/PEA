# PEA
This repository contains the LaTeX source files for my research work on corrosion protection of aluminium-silicon-copper alloys, mainly AC46000, using Layered Double Hydroxides (LDH) and corrosion inhibitors, conducted at the University of Aveiro.
## Research Overview
The study focuses on ZnAl-LDH as the primary LDH system, investigating its pre-treatment conditions and growth parameters on Al-Si-Cu alloys. Several corrosion inhibitors are evaluated for incorporation into the LDH structure, including vanadates, molybdates, MBT, BTA, L-cystine, and 8-HQ.
Corrosion protection efficiency is assessed through Electrochemical Impedance Spectroscopy (EIS) and further analyzed using Equivalent Circuit Modelling (ECM).
